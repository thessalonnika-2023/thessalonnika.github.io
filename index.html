About Us

At Thessalonnika Labour Relations Consulting, we understand that the employer–employee relationship sits at the heart of every successful organisation. Our name carries the spirit of collaboration, clarity and fairness — qualities we bring to every client engagement, whether you are a growing SME or an established enterprise. We are a specialist labour relations consultancy based in South Africa, dedicated to helping employers and employees navigate the full landscape of labour law with confidence, dignity and practical sense. We have entensive experience working with business, government and trade unions. Our expertise are locally and internationally embedded having worked at the International Labour Organisation (ILO) and contributing my creating a Practical Guide: Know the Workplace and its Relations.

What We Help You With — A Complete Labour Relations Framework

We leave nothing behind. Our services cover every stage and every aspect of the employment relationship, from hiring to exit and everything in between. The services below are designed to support employers and employees in a straightforward and professional way.

Labour Relations Advisory

Guidance on employee discipline, misconduct, conflict management, and everyday workplace relations concerns.

CCMA Preparation

Assistance with preparing documents, understanding the process, and getting ready for hearings or referrals. Representation at conciliation, mediation and arbitration hearings

Disciplinary Hearings

Support with fair disciplinary procedures, hearing structure, and workplace decision-making. Chairing or representing parties at disciplinary hearings, appeal hearings and incapacity inquiries

Workplace Policies

Drafting and reviewing workplace policies and procedures that help businesses stay organised and compliant. Drafting and reviewing employment contracts, restraint of trade agreements, confidentiality agreements and fixed‑term contracts. Developing legally sound HR policies and workplace codes of conduct — including disciplinary codes, grievance procedures, leave policies, and health & safety protocols

HR Compliance Support

Practical help with labour law compliance, workplace process, and employer responsibilities. Assisting employees and employers with formal grievance procedures. Mediating interpersonal or team conflicts before they escalate. Facilitating internal investigations and evidence evaluation

Employer Guidance

Advice for owners and managers who need a calm and clear path through difficult employee issues. Workshops for managers on conducting fair disciplinary hearings. Training for HR teams on CCMA preparedness and evidence management. Employee induction sessions on workplace policies, codes of conduct and grievance procedures.
