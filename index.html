About Us

Thessalonnika Labour Relations Consulting, founded and managed professionally by Joseph Motsepe (Labour Expert / Interventionist), exists to help employers and employees navigate workplace relations with confidence, building fair and practical labour relations platforms. We understand that the employer–employee relationship sits at the heart of every successful organisation.

Our purpose

We support employers, employees and organisations in managing workplace issues before they become bigger legal or disciplinary problems. The focus is on fairness, compliance, and practical solutions that help protect both the business and the people working in it.

Ideal for: small businesses, NGOs, schools, and organisations that need affordable guidance.

  • Employers handling staff disputes and employees needing advice and counselling
  • Businesses needing basic labour compliance support
  • Organisations and workers preparing for workplace hearings
  • Clients who need professional advice without unnecessary complications
